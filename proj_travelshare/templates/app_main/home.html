A FREE webapp that provides the platform for travelers to share their experience, stories and skills with the locals, in the form of a talk/speech, series of workshops or others. On the other hand, locals could benefit from these kind of on-site events while local organizations act like the localhosts to help make this happen by organizing those events. This platform is a dedicated virtual space that aiming to make this exchange as easy as possible.

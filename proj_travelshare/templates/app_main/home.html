Travel To Share

A FREE webapp that provides the platform for travelers to share their experience, stories and skills with the locals, in the form of a talk/speech, series of workshops or others. Meanwhile, local organizations perform the role of localhosts to help organize those events.

This site is a dedicated virtual space that aiming to make this exchange of information as easy as possible. Therefore, locals could benefit from those on-site events offered by travelers.
